The International Terminology Summer School explores exciting possibilities in terminology, translation, scientific and technical writing, and artificial intelligence. Our program is tailored for doctoral students, researchers, and language industry professionals seeking to stay ahead of the game.

Program outline

Come and join us for an exciting 30-hour program featuring a diverse panel of global experts who will provide you with master classes, workshops, seminars, and personalized tutoring. This program will cover the latest advancements in the field, making it an unmatched learning opportunity.

PhD project presentation sessions and tutorials

Our summer school includes presentations (pitching format) of the doctoral investigation projects of the participating students, followed by critical feedback from the professors chosen and invited for this purpose.

Social and cultural program

The Summer School will organize various cultural activities.
